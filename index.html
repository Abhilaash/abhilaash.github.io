CGH Technologies: I made a mobile application for a government subcontractor that displays NOTAMs (Notices to Airmen) in a manner that is easier to read. NOTAMs are used to notify aircraft about any issues at the airport. Generally NOTAMs can take even a skilled reader a minute to decipher, however, my application can parse it and make it easy enough for even the general public to understand in seconds. I was able to present this at the AIXM conference.
